# Learning AI in Public

A developer's honest diary of going AI-first. Starting from the basics, learning in public, and documenting every win, mistake, and "aha" moment along the way.

If you're an engineer who feels behind on the AI wave, this is for you. No pretending to be an expert. Just real notes from someone figuring it out.

## About

I'm [Danial Betres](https://www.linkedin.com/in/danbet/), a software engineer who works with TypeScript, React Native, React, and Ruby on Rails. I've been using AI tools casually for a while, but I recently committed to being AI-first in my workflow. This repo is me documenting that journey from the start.

## Who This Is For

- Engineers who feel behind on AI tooling
- Developers who use AI but feel like they're only scratching the surface
- Anyone tired of only hearing from the "10x AI power users" and wanting to see a beginner's honest perspective
